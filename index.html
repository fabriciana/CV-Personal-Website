Loftgarten is specialises in creating high-end spatial experiences through creative direction, 3D and architectural visualisations, motion design, and virtual reality. We showcased the breathtaking 3D art by Paul Milinski through a based-magazine editorial design and WebGL effects.

Story

The work has aimed to push the lighthouse of 3D spatial design, towards the boundless design possibilities WebGL and editorial design allows us. Together with the highly skilled in-house team at Loftgarten, we teamed up to find the perfect typography balance and a straigthforward editorial design to promote the indoor and outdoor spaces created by Paul Milinski.
