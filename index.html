About me

I am a programmer and software developer based out of NYC with 7+ years of experience designing and constructing responsive and interactive website, with proficiency in a myriad of both front and back-end languages. I have competed in various hackathons and has collaborated with professionals within educational organizations, such as Upperline Code and Flatiron School.

In addition, I has experience creating curriculum and making topics in CS and AI/Machine learning more accessible in partnership with nonprofit organizations to underserved middle and upper school students in urban communities. I especially find streamlining opportunities to students in need meaningful, and am passsionate about the bridge between human interaction and digital interfaces.
