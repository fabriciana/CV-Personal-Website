A bit about myself

Hi! As I've said before, welcome, my name's Fabriciana De Soriano and I am a Senior in High School, living in New York City.

A resume or CV is of course, meant to be formal, but I wanted to take some time to introduce myself, outside of whatever capacity you're meeting me or examining my credentials in :)

I also love coding in my free time (who would've guessed!); it's definitely become a way for me to bring both my analytical and more creative annd design centered passions together!
